F.A.Q

  • What is a hackathon?

    It's a place where programmers, engineers, designers, and entrepreneurs come together to build an awesome project in a short amount of time. We provide the venue and food; you bring the creativity. Get ready to learn, build something cool, and meet new people, while competing for prizes.

  • What if I’m a beginner?

    TeenHacks HTX encourages students of all levels to come! In addition, we will be hosting a pre-Hackathon introductory camp and workshop weeks before the event begins, as well as conducting workshops during the event.

  • Who can attend?

    All high school students are welcome to attend. If you are older or younger and still want to participate as a hacker, shoot us an email. If you're older, we welcome you to sign-up as a mentor!

  • Does it cost anything?

    TeenHacks HTX is only 10$ to attend, thanks to our generous sponsors! This will include lunch, swag, and an entry in our raffle for a few awesome prizes, as well as - of course - the potential to win our top prizes for coding the best project.

  • What if I don't have a team?

    In the beginning, we’ll have a team formation session to make sure you’re not alone. You can have teams as large or small as you want, but only teams of 3-5 will be eligible for prizes. Feel free to also assemble teams of your own before arriving.

  • What should I bring?

    You should bring your Student ID or other form of photo-identification, laptop, phone, chargers, ticket from EventBrite, as well as any hardware or materials you may need for your project. Nothing hazardous or illegal.
