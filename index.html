About
the
Event

What

A free, fun and engaging 24-hour virtual hackathon open to all high schoolers.

When

6 PM on May 29th to 6 PM on May 30th

Where

Online, hosted on our Discord server and streamed via Twitch. Register to receive the details of how to join.

Event Schedule

6 - 7 PM

Check-in

Make sure you've registered online. We'll send out an email to your registered email in the days leading up to the event with instructions on joining. If you don't have a team, use this time to connect with other hackers and form one.

7 - 8 PM

Opening Ceremony

Some introductory notes, competition guidelines, and basic rules of conduct. We'll also introduce our speakers, workshops, booths, organizers, and judges.

8 PM to 12 AM

Coding Session One

Start working!

12 AM - 7 AM

Coding Session Two / Sleep

You can continue to work through this time, but it's highly recommended that you get some sleep :)

7 AM - 8 AM

Breakfast Break / Games

Take a break to eat breakfast. We'll open up some mini games on our Discord server for you to play and connect with other hackers.

8 AM - 12 PM

Coding Session Three

Keep working! Workshops will also be held during this time.

12 PM - 1 PM

Lunch Break

Take a break to eat! We'll open the gaming channels during this time again.

1 PM to 3 PM

Coding Session Four

This is the final stretch! Make sure your projects are submitted (and in their final presentation form) to Devpost by 3:00.

3 PM - 5 PM

Workshops and Speakers

Enjoy workshops, lessons, and speakers while you wait for your submission to be graded.

5 PM - 6 PM

Closing Ceremony

We'll reveal the winning teams and wrap up the competition. We'll mail our swag and the winning prizes afterwards.

Competition
Details

Teams

Teams of 2-4 coders each.
Sign up on our Registration page.

Judging

Submissions will be judged by University of Houston CodeRED alumni at the end of the competition for creativity and skill.

Prizes

F.A.Q

  • What is a hackathon?

    It's a place where programmers, engineers, designers, and entrepreneurs come together to build an awesome project in a short amount of time. Get ready to learn, build something cool, and meet new people, while competing for prizes.

  • What if I’m a beginner?

    TeenHacksHTX encourages students of all levels to come! In addition, we will be conducting workshops during the event, and we'll always have a team member available to help you.

  • Who can attend?

    All high school students are welcome to attend. If you are older or younger and still want to participate as a hacker, shoot us an email.

  • Does it cost anything?

    TeenHacksHTX Spring 2020 is completely free thanks to donations from our generous sponsors. If you appreciate the work we do, however, we do take personal donations!

  • What if I don't have a team?

    In the beginning, we’ll have a team formation session to make sure you’re not alone. You can have teams as large or small as you want, but only teams of 2-4 will be eligible for prizes. We do recommend you to come with a team in mind already however - it'll save you time and effort - so encourage your friends and peers to register as well!

  • What kind of swag will I receive?

    Glad you asked! We'll be mailing out custom swag bags to every attendee, to the mailing address on the Registration form. Winners will additionally receive cash prizes and T-shirts from our sponsors.

  • 24 hours is kind of long - do I have to work the whole time?

    Of course not! Besides taking breaks to eat and sleep, we'll also be conducting minigames and challenges periodically.
